###Personas###
##1. The Proactive Giver: Sarah (The "Over-Shopper")##
Background: 34, busy marketing manager living in a suburban area. 
Motivation: Feels guilty throwing away perfectly good produce or leftovers after hosting weekend dinners. 
Pain Points: Finds existing donation processes too formal; wants a quick way to ensure food doesn't go to waste without the hassle of "selling." 
Goal: To list surplus items (like a bag of organic spinach or extra portions of lasagne) in under 60 seconds and find a reliable local recipient. 

##2. The Budget-Conscious Student: Leo (The "Resourceful Neighbor")##
Background: 21, international student living on a tight budget. 
Motivation: Looking for ways to save on groceries while maintaining a healthy diet. Pain Points: Limited transport options; needs to find food within walking or cycling distance. 
Goal: To browse a map or list of available items nearby and coordinate a safe, transparent pickup time that fits his class schedule.
